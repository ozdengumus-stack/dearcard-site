Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

DearCard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information entered by the user is stored locally on the user's device only.

2. Sender Name

Users may optionally enter a sender name when sharing a card. This name is stored only on the device and is never transmitted to DearCard's servers or shared with any third party.

3. Card Content

Cards created in DearCard are generated entirely on the user's device. DearCard does not view, store, or process any card content.

4. Third-Party Services

Card sharing is handled through the user's chosen messaging or social media applications. The privacy policies of those applications are outside the scope of DearCard's responsibility.

5. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please use our contact form.
